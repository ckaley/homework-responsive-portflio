About Me

Craig Grew up in Wisconsin and went to school at University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee for undergraduate studies in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. Becuase we is an avid skier, he felt he needed to move to Colorado and chase the snow. Wisconsin was mainly cold and not always much snow.

Craig moved to Colorado in 1992 and worked in the telecom indurstry as an IT professional. He went back to school to obtain and MBA. Craig has always loved learning new thigns and even taught at the college level after he obtained his masters degree.

On a personal side, Craig is married to Karen for 27 years and counting. He has three wonderful kids, Scott age 25, Nicole age 23 and Tyler age 21. They all share Craig's interest in sports and outdoor activities
